## 🚀 How to Get Started

1. Go to [AIEnhancer](https://aienhancer.ai/)  
2. Upload your image (old, blurry, or low-res).  
3. Choose your settings: resolution, denoise level, style filter.  
4. Click **Generate** and watch AIEnhancer work its magic.  
5. Download your HD image and share anywhere!
